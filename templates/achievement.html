Recent Activities

Presenting at ICT-CEEL 2023

Presented our paper, “Automation of Driving License Test Using Computer Vision and Image Processing,” at the International Conference on Technologies for Computer, Electrical, Electronics & Communication (ICT-CEEL 2023), held by Khwopa Engineering College and IEEE PES from October 11–13, 2023. Received the Best Paper Award for this work.

Winning All Nepal Hackathon 2023

Led Vision Architects in a three-day All Nepal Hackathon in the e-Governance category. Our project automated the driving license test for four-wheelers using computer vision, enhancing efficiency for Nepal’s Department of Transport. Secured first place in the competition.

National TV Interview on Kantipur Television

Featured on Kantipur Television as team leader of Vision Architects after winning the All Nepal Hackathon 2023. Discussed our e-Governance project automating driving license tests with computer vision, highlighting its impact and innovation.
